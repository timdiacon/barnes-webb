Adopt Bees

About Us

We're Barnes & Webb - a London based beehive rental company set up in 2013 by Chris Barnes and Paul Webb. We're passionate about bees and urban beekeeping and we've found a way of sharing this experience and the honey with others.

We've been placing hives across the capital building an urban apiary that's not only giving people local honey & pollinated gardens but also giving them a unique way to reconnect with nature. All this coupled with the knowledge that together we're collectively doing something towards relieving the plight of the honey bee.

We're BBKA certified beekeepers, registered with DEFRA and members of both the BBKA and LBKA.

The Shop

We only sell raw honey, produced and harvested by hand in London. There’s nothing changed and nothing added. It's simply filtered and jarred. Raw honey contains active anti-bacterial ingredients that can help the immune system, the heart and build immunity to seasonal allergies such as hay fever. It tastes amazing too.
